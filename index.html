The Pig Game

"The Pig Game" is a classic dice-based game designed for two players. Players take turns rolling a dice and accumulating points in their current round. The catch is that if a player rolls a 1 on the dice, their current round's points are lost, and it becomes the other player's turn. To safeguard their points, players can choose to "hold," transferring their current round's points to their total score. The first player to reach a specified score (often 100) wins the game. With luck and strategy in play, "The Pig Game" offers a delightful blend of chance and decision-making for an engaging and entertaining gaming experience.
